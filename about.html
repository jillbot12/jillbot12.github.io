ABOUT

Modern Primitive Exchange (MPEx) is a forum and a filing cabinet for ideas, projects and resources for living through the end times. We assume that the end times are already here (always!) and that something new is beginning (always!). We are neutral on Heidegger’s question concerning technology, which simply put is: to what extent can technology be a saving power and to what extent must it be a danger? We look to mediate between extreme responses to this question that continue to plague the environmental movement.No more mythology of the modern! No more mythology of the primitive! Let’s put it in all in conversation. We invite you to share your projects, resources and ideas on these themes as well. In the process we hope to learn some actual skills.

We file all of our content into one of the following bins, which are available as content filters on the site:


Tribe

What we are together; how we include and exclude.


Food

What we eat and drink (including water); how we grow it, how we sell it.


Shelter

What we build; how we make ourselves safe and comfortable.


Magic

What we use to transform; how we harness the fourfold (earth, sky, divinities, mortals)

what we are

What is Modern?

The fantasy of progress.
The achievement of networks, efficiency and sanitation.

What is Primitive?

The fantasy of purity.
The achievement of simplicity, resource management and respect for the non-human.

Why Exchange?

To re-write the past
and openthe future.
To combine myths and tools.
