Contributions

Modern Primitive Exchange is interested in any project, event, piece of writing or image that helps us explore what of the Modern and what of the Primitive is useful and/or a fantasy. We believe neither in “getting back to nature” nor in a blind faith in technology. We welcome the contibution of instructional projects and resources directly to this site via the upload page.
